Push/Pull/Legs Split Program

6 days per week

Program Overview

The Push/Pull/Legs split is a popular training method that organizes workouts by movement patterns:

  • Push: All pushing movements that train the chest, shoulders, and triceps
  • Pull: All pulling movements that train the back, rear delts, and biceps
  • Legs: Lower body exercises for quads, hamstrings, glutes, calves, and core

This program is ideal for intermediate to advanced lifters who can commit to training 5-6 days per week, allowing for maximum muscle isolation and recovery between sessions.

Target Muscles

  • Chest (pectoralis major & minor)
  • Shoulders (anterior & lateral deltoids)
  • Triceps (all three heads)

Pull Day

  • Rear Deltoids
  • Biceps & Forearms

Legs Day
