Bro Split Program

5-6 days per week
Program Overview

The Bro Split is a bodybuilding classic that focuses on training individual muscle groups on separate days. This approach provides:

  • High volume training for each muscle group
  • 7 days between training the same muscles
  • Focus on isolation exercises
  • Ideal for advanced lifters

Best suited for experienced lifters who can handle high training volumes and want maximum focus on individual muscle groups.
